The Power of E5 Technology

Imagine a scenario where you have a health condition that requires urgent surgery, but there is no specialist doctors nowhere near you. What if they could operate on you from a distance? Or more simply, picture yourself downloading the latest Rick and Morty episode to watch. What if that only took a few seconds? All these things, and many more, could become our day-to-day reality with the power of 5G internet. What is 5G and why it will be revolutionary? 5G stands for fifth-generation cellular wireless, where the “G” actually means “generation” and it promises to be faster and more furious than ever before. It is expected to connect people, things, data, applications, transport systems and cities in smart networked communication environments. 5G network should transport a huge amount of data much faster, reliably connect an extremely large number of devices and process very high volumes of data with minimal delay. It will allow devices to download data at more than 1 gigabit per second (1gb/s) which is at last ten to hundred times faster than today’s 4G services. Countries like the United States, China, South Korea, and the United Kingdom have 5G available in some areas. It’s predicted that the connectivity 5G technology delivers will revolutionize everything, from our health and wellbeing to transport and manufacturing processes and even Smart Cities.
